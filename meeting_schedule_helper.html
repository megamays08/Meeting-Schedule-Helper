Meeting Schedule Helper

Build recurring meeting series against a list of dates. Custom Holidays and other "NO-GO" dates can be entered as dates to avoid (hard stops), others can be entered as busy periods which are flagged so you can decide if you want to move the date. You can build multiple series in the tool and after you finalize your meetings, you can export the dates to CSV or a ICS calendar format. If adding new calendar dates, download a copy of the current calendar from the tool, change/add dates, then reupload.
Your series are saved automatically in this browser.
